# Separation of Ownership and Control

**Authors::** Eugene F. Fama, Michael C. Jensen
**Year::** 1983
**Publication::** Journal of Law and Economics
**URL::** 

## Abstract
Explains how organizations survive the separation of residual risk bearing from decision management. Distinguishes decision management (initiation and implementation) from decision control (ratification and monitoring), and argues that boards and other control devices reduce agency problems when residual claimants are diffuse.

## Reading Notes
> Decision management and decision control are separated in complex organizations.

**Note:** Use this split when coding interview themes on board ratification of related-party transactions.

> The board of directors is the common apex of the decision control system.
